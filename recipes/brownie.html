Brownies

Ingredients

  • Sugar; Two cups
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Mix Ingredients in a bowl
  2. Spread batter into a pan
  3. Bake in preheated until top is dry
  4. Take out brownies and let it cool
  5. Cut the brownies into squares
